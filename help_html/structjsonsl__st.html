u-Escape handling

Special handling for the \u-f00d type sequences. These are meant to be translated back into the corresponding octet(s). A special callback (if set) is invoked with *at=='u'. An application may wish to temporarily suspend parsing and handle the 'u-' sequence internally (or not).
